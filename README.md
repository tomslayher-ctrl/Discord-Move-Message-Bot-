Save the Vibe: Don't kill a conversation just because it's in the wrong channel. Move it and keep it going.

Instant Cleanup: Automatically deletes the original message so your channels stay spotless.

No Commands: Your staff doesn't have to learn a single command. It just works natively within Discord.

✨ Key Features
Identity Mirroring: Preserves original user avatars and names via webhooks so the conversation looks exactly the same in its new home.

Media Ready: Moves text, images, videos, and files without losing a single pixel.

Right-Click Native: No prefix commands to memorize; fully integrated into the Discord "Apps" menu.

Privacy First: We don't log, store, or track your messages. Ever.

🛠️ How to Use
Right-click any message.

Select Apps → Move Message.

Choose the destination channel. Done.

⚠️ Important Setup
[!IMPORTANT]
Role Hierarchy: To ensure zero issues, move the Movr role to the very top of your server's Role Hierarchy.

Discord prevents bots from managing messages from users with a higher role. Placing Movr at the top ensures it can relocate content from anyone, including Admins and Moderators.
